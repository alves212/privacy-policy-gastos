Privacy Policy for Gastos

1. Overview

This privacy policy explains how the Gastos app handles your personal data. Gastos is a personal finance tracker built for individual use. Your privacy is important, and this app is designed to minimize data collection and maximize transparency.

2. Data Collection

We collect only the data you explicitly provide within the app:

  • Email and password for authentication (handled securely via Firebase Authentication).
  • Financial entries you manually add (e.g., incomes, expenses).

3. Data Storage

All user data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore, under a private and isolated user account. Each user can access only their own data.

4. Data Usage

Your data is used solely to power the core features of the Gastos app:

  • Displaying your financial data
  • Syncing your data across devices
  • Allowing you to manage your entries (add, update, delete)

We do not use your data for advertising or analytics.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. All data remains private and accessible only to you.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Delete your account and associated data at any time from the app
  • Contact the developer to request data deletion or for other privacy concerns

7. Security

We follow industry best practices and rely on Firebase’s security standards to protect your data. Access to user data is strictly controlled via security rules.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this privacy policy if required. All changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
